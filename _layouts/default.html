Hello, World!

School of Machines, Making & Make-Believe is an art & technology school and non-profit organisation in Berlin, Germany, whose mission is to bring together artists and creatives working with technology and technologists working within the context of art, in an intensive, summer learning program. We invite a community of like-minded artists, coders and makers together to creatively explore craft in-depth with a focus on individual self-expression and storytelling.

Summer 2014, we'll be running two one-month full-time programs, for 10-15 students each. Our July 2014 program will focus on creative coding and interactive installations in the context of experimental storytelling. Our August 2014 program will be focused on making+motors, digital fabrication & making things move. Check out a full description of our summer programs.

Hosted by The WYE.

In collaboration with FablabBerlin and OpenTechSchool.
With support from ArtHackDay, Retune, Studio NAND, Onformative, TinkerSoup, Berlin Geekettes, and Create Digital Motion.
