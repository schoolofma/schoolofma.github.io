School of Machines, Making & Make-Believe is a non-profit organisation whose mission is to bring together artists working with technology and technologists working within the context of art, in an intensive, summer learning program in Berlin, Germany. We invite a community of like-minded artists, coders and makers together to creatively explore craft in-depth with a focus on individual self-expression, storytelling and narrative.

Summer 2014, School will run two one-month full-time programs, for 10-15 students each. July 2014 program will focus on creative coding and interactive installations in the context of experimental storytelling. August 2014 program will be focused on making+motors, fabrication & making things move. Check out full description of our summer programs.

Hosted by The WYE.

In collaboration with FablabBerlin, OpenTechSchool, and with support from ArtHackDay, Retune, Berlin Geekettes.
