Hello World!

School of Machines, Making & Make-Believe is an organisation dedicated to the imaginative and non-sensical exploration of art and technology. We’re passionate about interactivity and storytelling. We enjoy learning and playing with tools that make our brains and our dreams come to life! Wanna join us?

We invite creative people of all kinds -- artists, technologists, makers, engineers, designers, the curious, people passionate about learning and creativity, those who secretly desire to create something meaningful -- all are encouraged to apply.

Fabricating Empathy

  • / 20 April - 22 May 2015

  • / five weeks, full-time in Belgrade, Serbia

  • / € 1600* (see FAQ)

How can we create meaningful experiences using technology to express who we are and where we came from? This course will focus on Empathy and Culture in the context of art and technology. While learning digital fabrication techniques, designing mechanisms and kinetic sculptures as a way to animate the invisible forces that shape our human behaviour, we’ll simultaneously explore Empathy as a necessary tool to strengthen human connection and enhance our experiences of life.

The goal of the program is to explore in-depth the creative and expressive possibilities of digital fabrication (3D printers, CNC) and kinectic objects while delving into speculative and critical design. In addition, we’ll also have an opportunity to explore Oculus Rift and ways in which it helps us to extend our empathic capabilities outside ourselves and into public spaces.

Teachers and students will work together towards the production of individual and group projects and will have access to the equipment needed to create functioning kinectic installations. You're going to have a great time delving into machinery, electronics and code and experimenting with your heart and your hands.
