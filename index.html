School of Machines, Making & Make-Believe brings together artists working with technology andhackers working with art, in an intensive, selfdirected summer learning program to creatively explore their craft in-depth within a community of likeminded curious thinkers and makers!

Summer 2014, School of Machines, Making & Make-Believe will run two one-month full-time programs, for 10-15 students each.

July 2014 will be focused on Making+Motors: Fabrication & Making Things Move.

Our August 2014 program will focus on creative coding, interactive public installation and projection-mapping.

Classes will be a mix of lectures, workshops, labs and experiments with well-known artists, hackers and educators. Students will also have opportunities to share their knowledge with each other through student-led workshops.
