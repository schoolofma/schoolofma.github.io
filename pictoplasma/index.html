  • / #charactech contest ends 14. May 2018

  • / In collaboration with Pictoplasma Character Design Festival

Pictoplasma and The School of Machines, Making, and Make-Believe come together for the #CharaTech Contest: an initiative to unify character designers around the purpose of experimenting with interactive technology. The winner is offered a FREE MASTERCLASS of their choosing at the School of Machines. You can pick any 2018 summer course or wait for our 2019 programs.

The challenge is to view your characters through the lens of creative and interactive technology. Entrants have four frames to tell a story that places their characters in a new world inspired by the upcoming programs of our school.
