School of Machines, Making & Make-Believe is a non-profit organisation whose mission is to bring together artists working with technology and hackers working within the context of art, in an intensive, summer learning program in Berlin, Germany. We invite a community of like-minded artists, coders and makers together to creatively explore craft in-depth with a focus on individual self-expression, storytelling and narrative.

Summer 2014, School of Machines, Making & Make-Believe will run two one-month full-time programs, for 10-15 students each.
