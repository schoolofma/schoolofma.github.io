Join us for a four-week pop-up co-working/co-creation space this June. Along with other creatives, you'll have an opportunity to work with like-minded people, gain access to tools, organise workshops, and more based in our temporary studio/gallery space in Berlin, Mitte!

We've got the ACUD Studio and ACUD Gallery from 5th - 30th of June and we're opening it up to extend our creative community here in Berlin. Along with a space to work, you'll also have access to the space for organising your own workshops and events. Have an idea for a course? Need help with planning and execution of your event idea? We'll help!

You'll also have access to use of our tools this month! Interested in getting into art and technology, wanna play around with computer vision and VR with others? We've got tools and eager participants, join someone else's project or work on your own!

Who is this co-working/co-creation space for?

This is aimed at artists, creatives, designers, and anyone interested in working for four-weeks amongst Berlin's creative technology community.

Available materials include:

-ps eye cameras
-kinects
-arduinos
-3D printer
-raspberry pis
-VR (google cardboard, oculus dk1's, VIVE)
-projectors
